 TinyCircuits Watch

A TinyCircuits Smart Watch Kit was transformed into a fitness tracker, which could automatically detects when a repetition of Push Up/Sit Up was done. The aim of the fitness tracker it to help users to keep track of their repetition count such that their focus could be entirely on the exercise and to display a pace to achieve the maximum score for IPPT. The motion is detected through the accelerometer sensor, and a score calculator helps the user to calculate the amount of points needed to achieve the gold award. Information from the fitness tracker could be sent to mobile devices through Bluetooth Low Energy.

Tools/Languages: Arduino (C)
